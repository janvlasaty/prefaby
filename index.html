by Dominik Migač and Jakub Maksymov

one of a kind metapuppet performance

Prefaby is a technological marvel.
A precisely deatiled and humbly intimate spectacle.
Miniature robotics, 5 channel sound, videomapping.
7 audience members. 1 actor.

story

A story from a prefabricated flat building - a symbol of a fullfiled dream of modern living in the former Eastern Bloc. Brutalistic architecture. Freely variated motifs of everyday life of 3 neighbors. Everything is set on a formic table. A pre-apocalyptic concrete idyll referring to a tragical crash in Chernobyl.
